Campus Information

The College campus is situated in palur hills and is based on several buildings, all next to each other. The buildings are spacious with facilities that enable students to enjoy a successful academic and social life. The campus comprises of a library, laboratories, classrooms, a gym and a cafeteria and administration and faculty offices.
